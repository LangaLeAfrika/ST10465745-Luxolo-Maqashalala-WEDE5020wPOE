Our Services

Eyethu Shopping Center offers essential services designed for convenience and community growth in Joe Slovo, making it a true all-in-one destination.

  • **Eyethu Filling Station** – Affordable and reliable fuel, complemented by a quick-shop for essential convenience items, serving commuters and locals 24/7.
  • **Eyethu Superstore** – Your local supermarket providing a comprehensive range of groceries, fresh produce, and household essentials in a community-focused setting.
  • **Retail Shops** – Eight rental spaces available for diverse businesses like fashion, electronics, and fast food, providing opportunities for local entrepreneurs and variety for customers.
